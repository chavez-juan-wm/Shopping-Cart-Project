Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add items to my cart?
A: Navigate to the products page, once there click on the image of the product you wish to add to your cart. Once you click on the image, you will see a button named: "Add to Cart". Click the button to add the product to your cart.
Q: How do I change the quantity of a particular item in my cart?
A: First, click on "Cart" in the navigation bar to view your cart. This will allow you to view all of the items currently in your cart, as well as the quantities that you have chosen for each item. To change the quantity of an item in your cart, move your cursor to the box that appears under the "Quantity" header for this item and type in the quantity desired, then click the “Update” button next to the box. Once you do this, the quantity and associated dollar amount will automatically change and reflect the correct amounts for both the quantity and the cost.
Q: How do I remove items from my cart?
A: First, click on "Cart" in the navigation bar to view your cart. This will allow you to view all of the items currently in your cart. Once you have identified the item that you would like to remove, click on “Delete" next to that item.
Q: How do I checkout?
A: First, click on "Cart" in the navigation bar to view your cart. This will allow you to view all of the items currently in your cart. At the bottom of the page, there is a button called: "Checkout". Once you click it, enter your billing details, enter your payment method, and purchase your products. You will receive a receipt once your purchase goes through.
Q: Is ordering online secure?
A: Yes. We take the utmost care with the information that you provide us when placing an order on our website.
Q: What are your payment options?
A: We accept four types of major credit cards (Mastercard, Visa, American Express, and Discover).
Q: If I'm not satisfied with what I purchased, can I get a refund?
A: Sadly, that is not possible. Once you buy a product you can't return it and get a refund.
